Tekken

  Buy On Pancakeswap


Tekken token is our first token and allows users to hold Millions of them. It is an experiment in decentralized spontaneous community building. This BEP-20 ONLY token can remain well under a penny and still outpace Shiba in a small amount of time (relatively speaking).

Tekken is entirely community run, there is no centralized team. All social profiles are started by community members.

We locked the 100% of the total supply to Pancake Swap and threw away the keys (renounced contract ownership)! So everyone has to buy on the open market, ensuring a fair and complete distribution where devs don't own team tokens they can dump on the community. If you missed out on $DOGE and $SHIB here is your chance to get $Tekken early!

Tekken Info

The only place to buy or sell Tekken safely is on Pancakeswap. There are some centralized exchanges that will possibly list Tekken, but CEXs have a history of locking funds. If you can, it is safer to trade on a decentralized exchange. You can see your transactions on BscScan. Our contract code has been verified and is secure.
